# The Night before the dawn

An evening of chaos,
full of anarchy and weights.
The unforeseen, like a sudden summer downpour,
thieving the heaviness.
The sun sets with a sorrow,
agony and lament bleeding through the sky.

Here comes the night,
gloomy to its infinity.
Evening was of rush, night is of placidity,
honouring the weight the heart must carry.
Walking with the destiny,
accepting the jest of life.

In the shadow of grief, night dwellers breathe,
playing the symphony of crickets.
With a breeze of snow queen,
there is a tranquility of the midst black night.
Nature has its own plans,
to embrace the process, not to walk with burdens.

Alas, Here is the Dawn! 
Pearls of Dew jewelled on the dancing leaves.
Fall and Rise of the Marianne,
Building a new civilization on the past debris.
Across the woods, the chirping of sparrows, the calls of peacocks,
here is how the night before the dawn glorifies daylight.
